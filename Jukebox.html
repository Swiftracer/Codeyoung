Jukebox with Different Genres of Music

In this jukebox, you have five songs from five different genres of music. These genres of music are classical, pop, jazz, reggae, and hip-hop. I hope you enjoy!


Für Elise - Classical Genre

Für Elise is composed by the famous composer Ludwig van Beethoven. It was created in 1979. You may think it is a very old song, but we consider it a classic. It is an all-time song and I hope you thoroughly enjoy this song.



Blinding Lights - Pop Genre

Blinding Lights was created in 2020 by the artist called, the Weeknd. It has been a major hit in the US and has blown up all over the world. This came in the album called After Hours. Fact: The Weeknd is a Canadian singer.



Elevator Ride - Jazz Genre

The creator of Elevator Ride is David Renda. It is not a song at all, it was just a bunch of elevator music combined together. But when you really hear it, you will feel relaxed. Many of you may feel it sounds like music that plays while you are in the elevator (after all, the title of the song is named by it), but it is a very distinct type of Jazz.



24K Magic - Reggae Genre

You may know this better than the previous song, it is created by Bruno Mars after all. This artist released this song in 2016 in the album of 24K Magic. Reggae is not a genre many people know but here is a summed up definition of it. Reggae is a music genre that originated in Jamaica in the late 1960s. Reggae usually relates news, social gossip, and political commentary. If you are interested, you can find more about this in Wikipedia.


Industry Baby - Hip-Hop Genre

This song actually has two artists: Lil Nas X and Jack Harlow. It was recently released this year in the album of Montero. Many of you may not know that Lil Nas X's real name is Montero Lamar Hill. So strange, right?
