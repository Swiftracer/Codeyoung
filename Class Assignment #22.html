Steps for making sugar from a sugarcane:

  1. After harvesting the sugarcanes, clean the cane.
  2. Next, cut them into strips.
  3. Insert the strips through heavily grooved crusher rollers, to seperate the fibers from the juice.
  4. Dissolve the the stalks in hot water and let them boil. The sugar will eventually separate from the stalks.
  5. Filter the juice.
  6. You boil the juice at a certain temparature that is called vacuum evaporation. This will thicken the juice.
  7. The next step is to evaporate the concentrated syrup until it is saturated with sugar. You will add small grains called seed that will help crystalize the sugar.
  8. Keep adding more syrup, allowing the crystals to grow more until they fill up the pan.
  9. Separate the massecuite into raw sugar crystals and molasses, in which the crystals will get wet.


  10. It is then dried and packaged.
